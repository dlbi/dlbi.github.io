Akara Supratak, Ph.D

I am an Assistant Professor at the Faculty of ICT, Mahidol University. My current research focuses on advancing the field of biomedical informatics through the development and application of cutting-edge deep learning techniques. My goal is to find the best way to train deep learning models with limited data and resources for medical image and signal analysis.
